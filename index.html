Intrip's Future Mission

Our mission is to give all travelers worldwide access to the blockchain-based traveling platform Intrip to enable the convenience of paying with cryptocurrencies and getting loyalty rewards in cryptocurrencies, such as TripCoin, Bitcoin, and Ethereum, instead of useless points. Know more below.

01

Book Hotels

Intrip is a hotel booking aggregator that allows booking your destination hotel on your favorite platforms like booking.com or Airbnb via Intrip. You can pay with Cryptoassets (Tripcoin, Bitcoin, Ethereum, BNB, and MATIC), credit/debit cards, and PayPal.

Book Flights

Intrip is also a flight-booking aggregator that allows booking your flights on your favorite airlines, like Delta Airlines or AirAsia, via Intrip. You can pay with Cryptoassets (Tripcoin, Bitcoin, Ethereum, BNB, and MATIC), credit/debit cards, and PayPal.
